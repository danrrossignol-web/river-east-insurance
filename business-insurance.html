Common Commercial Insurance Policies

General Liability (GL)

The foundation of any business policy. It protects you against lawsuits for bodily injury (slip and falls) or property damage caused to others.

Business Owners Policy (BOP)

A cost-effective bundle that combines General Liability and Commercial Property coverage. Ideal for small to mid-sized businesses.

Commercial Auto

Coverage for your work trucks, delivery vans, and fleet vehicles. Essential for contractors and businesses with employees on the road.

Workers Compensation

Mandatory in CT for most businesses. Covers medical costs and lost wages for employees injured on the job.

Professional Liability (E&O)

Protects against claims of negligence, mistakes, or failure to deliver a service. Critical for consultants, doctors, and advisors.

Excess / Umbrella Liability

Provides additional liability limits above your GL and Auto policies. We can secure high-limit umbrellas to protect your assets from catastrophic lawsuits.

Contractor Bonds

From License & Permit bonds to Bid & Performance bonds. We help you meet municipal and contractual requirements.

Directors & Officers (D&O)

Protects the personal assets of corporate directors and officers against claims of wrongful acts in managing the company.

Key Person Life Insurance

A life insurance policy on a crucial owner or employee. It provides the business with capital to survive the loss of a key player.
