Essential Trucking Coverages

Primary Liability

Mandatory coverage. It pays for injuries or damage you cause to others. Most aggregate haulers require $1,000,000 in liability to get on major job sites.

Physical Damage

Protects your truck. If your dump truck tips over at a job site or hits a deer on Route 2, this pays to repair or replace your vehicle.

Motor Truck Cargo

Even if you are hauling dirt, you are responsible for the load. Cargo coverage protects you if the load is lost or dumped accidentally on the highway.

General Liability

Trucking liability only covers you while driving. General Liability covers you when you are loading, unloading, or if a customer slips at your yard.

Pollution Liability (Broadened)

If your truck overturns and spills diesel fuel into a wetland, the cleanup costs can be astronomical. Standard auto policies often exclude this. Ask us about adding it.
