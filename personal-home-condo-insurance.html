Understanding Your Policy

Standard Protections

Dwelling Coverage Protects the physical structure of your home (roof, walls, foundation) against fire, storms, and vandalism. We calculate "Replacement Cost" to ensure you can rebuild even if material prices rise.
Personal Property Covers the cost of replacing your belongingsâ€”furniture, electronics, clothingâ€”if they are damaged or stolen.
Liability Protection Protects you financially if someone is injured on your property or if you accidentally damage someone elseâ€™s property. This covers legal fees and settlements.
Additional Living Expenses (ALE) If a fire or storm makes your home uninhabitable, this pays for your hotel, food, and temporary living costs while repairs are made.

Critical Options & Extras

Water Backup & Sump Pump Critical in CT. Standard policies do NOT cover water backing up through sewers or drains. We strongly recommend adding this endorsement to protect finished basements.
Scheduled Personal Property Standard policies have limits on high-value items like jewelry, art, or guns. We can "schedule" these items to ensure they are covered for their full appraised value.
Flood Insurance Homeowners insurance does NOT cover floods. If you live near a water source, we can write a separate Flood Policy (NFIP or Private) to protect your home.
Umbrella Liability An extra layer of liability protection (usually $1M+) that kicks in when your home or auto limits are exhausted. Essential for protecting retirement savings.
