Essential Coverage for Contractors

General Liability (GL)

The foundation of your business. Protects you against third-party lawsuits for bodily injury or property damage caused by your work.

  • Damage to Customer Property
  • Completed Operations Coverage

Commercial Auto

Your truck is your office. Personal auto policies often exclude business use. We make sure your work vans and heavy trucks are covered.

Tools get stolen. It's a sad reality of the job. Inland Marine coverage pays to replace your tools and heavy equipment if they are stolen from a job site or vehicle.
