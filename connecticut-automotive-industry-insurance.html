Essential Coverage for Garages

Garagekeepers Liability

The most critical coverage. It covers damage to customer vehicles while they are in your care (e.g., test drives, left overnight, or on the lift).

  • Collision during Test Drives
  • Theft from Lot
  • Fire or Vandalism

Garage Liability

This is your "General Liability" but specifically for garage operations. It covers slip-and-falls in the waiting room or if a tire comes off a car after it leaves your shop causing damage.

  • Premises Liability
  • Products & Completed Operations
  • Faulty Workmanship (resulting damage)

Common Garage Insurance Questions

What is "Direct Primary" vs. "Legal Liability"?

Legal Liability only pays if the shop was negligent (you left the keys in the car). Direct Primary pays regardless of fault (a storm hits the car). We recommend Direct Primary to keep customers happy.

Are my employees' tools covered?

Not automatically. Business property covers your tools. We need to add an "Employee Tools" endorsement to cover the personal toolboxes of your mechanics.

Does my policy cover loaner cars?

If you give customers a loaner while their car is in the shop, you need specific "Auto Physical Damage" coverage for that loaner vehicle.

I sell a few cars a year. Am I a dealer?

If you sell vehicles, even occasionally, your standard Garage policy might exclude those sales. We can convert your policy to a "Garage & Dealer" form to cover both operations.
