Common Commercial Auto Questions

Can I just put my work truck on my personal policy?

Usually, no. If the vehicle is owned by an LLC or used primarily for business (especially if it has ladder racks or signage), most personal carriers will deny the claim. It needs a Commercial policy.

What impacts my Commercial Auto rate?

Driver age and record, vehicle weight/type, radius of operation (how far you drive), and the cargo you haul all play a major role.

Does this cover my tools?

No. Commercial Auto covers the vehicle. You need an "Inland Marine" (Tool Floater) endorsement to cover the expensive tools locked inside.

What if I have a bad driving record?

We have markets for high-risk drivers. It will cost more, but we can get you the filing you need to get back on the road legal.
