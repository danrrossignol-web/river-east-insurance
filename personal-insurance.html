More Than Just a Policy

Your insurance shouldn't just be a stack of papers you file away. It should be a cohesive strategy that protects your home, your assets, and your future earnings.

At River East Insurance, we specialize in building Total Protection Packages. Whether you own a historic home in Manchester or a lakeside property in Coventry, generic online quotes often leave dangerous gaps in coverage.

  • The Power of Bundling: We align your Home, Auto, and Umbrella policies with the same carrier when possible. This minimizes gaps and maximizes multi-policy discounts.
  • Asset Protection: For clients with substantial assets, a standard liability limit isn't enough. We help you calculate the right amount of Umbrella coverage to safeguard your savings.
  • Claims Advocacy: When a storm hits or an accident happens, you have a local advocate in your corner to guide you through the process.
