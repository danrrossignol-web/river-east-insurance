The "Gap" In Your Coverage

Most standard Auto and Homeowners policies in Connecticut cap their liability coverage at $300,000 or $500,000.

But what happens if you are sued for $1 Million or more?

If you are found at fault in a serious auto accident, or if someone is injured on your property, you are personally responsible for any damages that exceed your policy limits. This puts your home equity, retirement savings, and future wages at risk of garnishment.

An Umbrella Policy is designed to kick in exactly when your other policies run out, providing an extra layer of security for a surprisingly low annual cost.
